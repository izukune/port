While studying at Tokyo University of Science, I began working as a freelance web designer and developer. At the university, I belonged to the Department of Physics and was assigned to the laboratory of elementary particle theory. As the theme of my graduation thesis, I researched methods of fluid simulation using artificial intelligence for fluid phenomena in planetary meteorology. It was there that he first encountered artificial intelligence and realized the potential of deep learning in particular, and decided to pursue research in artificial intelligence in graduate school. Currently, I am working on the development of a highly accurate conversation engine using natural language processing at the Graduate School of Artificial Intelligence, Rikkyo University.
